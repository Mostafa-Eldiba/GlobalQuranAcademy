Recitation and Reflection: A Journey into the Heart of the Quran

The Quran, the eternal word of God, revealed to His Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) as guidance for humanity until the Last Day. To learn it is not merely to recite its words, but to receive its verses as if they were celestial melodies directly from the Lord of the Worlds. It is a manual for life, a comprehensive law for Muslims across all times and places. Therefore, memorizing the Quran and contemplating its meanings is a fundamental pillar in the making of a righteous Muslim.

The theme of the Course

We provide Quran Memorization Online course not only regarding the principles of memorizing the Holy Quran but also teach proper way of reciting the Holy Quran with Tajweed which is an essential factor that guarantees to recite Quran correctly. The course is mainly focused on the accurate pronunciation while memorizing the Quran.

Course description

This course, "Recitation and Reflection: A Journey into the Heart of the Quran," is designed for learners with little or no prior knowledge of the Quran. It begins by explaining the meaning of the word "Quran" and its significance as a source of guidance and inspiration. Our goal is to empower students to maximize their benefit from this course and gain a deep understanding of everything related to the Quran and the virtue of memorizing it in the life of every Muslim.

Objectives and Goals:

  • To memorize the Book of God accurately and with precision.
  • To recite the Quran with correct and fluent Tajweed.
  • To learn the meanings of the verses and the reasons for their revelation.
  • To understand the importance of memorizing the Quran in the life of every Muslim.
  • To connect the memorization of the Quran with understanding its meanings and applying them in daily life.
  • To emphasize the role of the Quran in organizing a Muslim's life and refining their conduct.
  • To illustrate the comprehensiveness of the Quran, as it contains principles, doctrines, and guidance for every aspect of human life.
  • To encourage every Muslim to exert all possible means to memorize the Quran, in obedience to the command of Allah and His Prophet (peace be upon him)

    • Who Should Enroll in This Course?

    Learning and memorizing the Quran is obligatory for every Muslim, regardless of age, gender, or nationality. This course is open to anyone who wishes to memorize the Quran or deepen their knowledge of the teachings of Allah Almighty.
