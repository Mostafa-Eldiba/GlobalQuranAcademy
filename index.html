Frequently AskedQuestions

What platform do you use to teach The Quran ?

We use Zoom, as it is much more secure than any alternatives and allows staff to monitor the classes.

Do you teach anything other than the Quran ?

No. To put it simply, we focus exclusively on the Quran and avoid teaching other subjects to children, as we specialise in refining the Quranic fluency of children. We do provide courses that teach children Duas; however, most of them are from the Quran.

Who uses Global Quran Academy ?

People of any age, living anywhere in the world can use Quran University at any time of the day that suits them: (any age, anywhere, & any time,). Even if you choose the teacher-supervised learning: you would still be able to learn at any time of your day, and you do not need to limit yourself to a specific appointment time to get in touch with your teacher… this is an innovative feature that we have invented that makes us unique and that would melt down the time zones differences. So no matter how busy your schedule is: you would always find the time to learn Quran.

Would I interact with my teacher alone, or share my sessions with a group of students ?

“Global Quran Academy” provides One-to-One interaction with your Quran teacher.

How does a study session with my teacher take place ?

The unit of study is based on assignments. Each assignment has a range of Quran to be recited or memorized in that session. A course is built of several sessions with an overall target to be achieved over a period of time. Let is us take an Example you want to memorize Chapter 30 of the Quran: Search and register for a course called “Chapter 30 Memorization”, select the speed of how much you can memorize per week (Do not worry if you don’t perform well, teacher can adjust that speed for you later on based on your actual performance capabilities) you can also select the language of your teacher and several other parameters that would guarantee the best match for you with the most suitable teacher) Once your application to the course is accepted by the teacher: your teacher would send you one or more assignments per week (depending on the speed you picked earlier). Upon receiving the assignment the student would go through self-learn phase using the Innovative software tools we have invented in our desktop & mobile applications. When the student reaches a state of self-satisfaction with his/her performance, student would send a recorded audio to teacher to correct.

Do you have female teachers ?

We only have female teachers as we strongly believe that women are very good with children. The women we have vetted are top-of-the-range tutors who possess teaching abilities that cater well to children!

How are your classes monitored ?

We have staff that will be active during your child’s lessons who will pop in to the online classes from time to time for safeguarding reasons.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time ?

Yes, you are not contractually locked in for a certain amount of time. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account portal. So, if you feel like you have learnt all you need, then you are free to stop further tutoring. Your subscription will be terminated from the time you cancel your reoccurring subscription. Please note that we do not offer refunds or cash back retrospectively from the time of cancelation.

Is there an age limit to take online Quran learning course ?

No there is no age limit. People of all age groups can apply for any course they are interested in.

Do you focus on teaching Quran to beginners or you also provide advanced Quran learning courses?

Online Quran Lesson focuses on online Quran learning for beginners as well as on teaching advanced Quran learning courses. You can view the details of the courses we provided on our website and register in the course of your liking.
